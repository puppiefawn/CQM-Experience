Ending

Saying a few things to Cheng Luan, you eventually bid it a farewell until next time, or until you and Liu-shidi are paired together again.

Redrawing your fingers from the blade, you gently push Cheng Luan toward Liu Chanshi and announcement to him you're finished.

"Thanks," he says, standing up. Cheng Luan is taken and slid back inside it's sheath. "I'm going." Taking his leave, Liu-shidi quickly walks out of the pavilion, set for his own peak.
